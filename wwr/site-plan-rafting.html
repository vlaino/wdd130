Purpose

Our purpose is give you best rafting experience. Living the funniest moment in your journey is our goal. Also we are specialist and very worry about your safe, because we have the most talent people, high quality equipment and vast experience in rapids. Audience

If you have an adventure spirit, like nature and adrenalin is part of your life, you can find here activities like rapids, with different challenges. But if you like to see the stars in the night, walk around the forest and look at many animals, we give you camping experience. All our activities give you the opportunity to connect with the nature and feel blessing for this beautiful world.
