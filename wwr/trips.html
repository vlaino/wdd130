Trips

River Gutierrez - Here the journey is twelve kilometers with moderate and intense rapids, and you will spend between fifty minutes and an hour fifteen of time in the water, depending on the seasonal flow, which increases considerably during the summer.

Camping - We leave for Aconcagua National Park, where we will begin the trekking to Confluencia, the first camp in Aconcagua. We have an approximate time of 4 hours ascent and 2 hours descent. Then we camped next to the river.

Cerros Negros has a route of approximately thirty kilometers within the Mendoza River, it is about two hours of water with a brief stop halfway so you can rest. None of these versions require prior experience or specific preparation!
